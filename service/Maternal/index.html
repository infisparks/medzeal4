Maternal Physiotherapy

Book Now

Our Maternal Physiotherapy services are dedicated to supporting women's physical well-being during pregnancy and throughout postnatal recovery. We focus on alleviating discomfort, enhancing posture, and strengthening the core and pelvic floor.

Tailored to your unique needs, our therapy programs combine gentle exercises, manual therapy, and relaxation techniques to improve mobility, reduce pain, and prepare your body for childbirth or aid in recovery after delivery.

Key Benefits

With our personalized approach, you can expect:

  • Relief from Pregnancy-related Discomfort
  • Improved Posture and Core Stability
  • Enhanced Pelvic Floor Strength
  • Reduced Back and Joint Pain
  • Faster Postnatal Recovery
  • Improved Overall Energy and Wellness
  • Customized Therapy Programs
  • Guidance for Safe Exercise Practices

Got Questions about Maternal Physiotherapy?

Our expert therapists are here to answer your queries and guide you through a safe and effective therapy journey during and after pregnancy.

Maternal Physiotherapy is a specialized rehabilitation service focused on addressing the physical challenges associated with pregnancy and postnatal recovery.

Expectant mothers and new mothers experiencing discomfort, posture issues, or pelvic floor weakness can greatly benefit from these specialized therapy programs.

A session may include gentle stretching, strengthening exercises, manual therapy, and relaxation techniques designed to support your changing body during and after pregnancy.

Our therapists conduct comprehensive assessments and monitor your progress closely to tailor exercises that are both safe and effective for your specific stage of pregnancy or recovery.
