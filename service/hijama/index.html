Cupping Therapy is an ancient healing practice where cups are applied to the skin to create suction. This therapy stimulates blood circulation, reduces muscle tension, and promotes overall wellness.

During cupping, specialized cups are placed on the skin to create a vacuum effect. This gentle suction helps to release toxins, improve circulation, and support the body’s natural healing process. Mild redness or bruising may occur, which is a normal and temporary reaction.
