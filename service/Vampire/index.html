Vampire Facial

Book Now

The Vampire Facial is an innovative skin rejuvenation treatment that harnesses the healing properties of your own platelet-rich plasma (PRP) to stimulate collagen production and promote a youthful, radiant complexion.

This minimally invasive procedure involves drawing a small amount of your blood, processing it to concentrate the platelets, and applying the PRP to your skin through microneedling techniques. The result is improved skin texture, reduced fine lines, and a natural glow.

Key Benefits

With the Vampire Facial, you can expect:

  • Enhanced Collagen Production
  • Improved Skin Texture and Tone
  • Reduction in Fine Lines and Wrinkles
  • Minimized Scarring and Hyperpigmentation
  • Natural and Youthful Glow
  • Personalized Treatment with Your Own PRP

Got Questions about Vampire Facial?

Our skincare experts are here to answer your questions and help you understand how the Vampire Facial can transform your skin.

A Vampire Facial is a PRP-based treatment that uses your own blood components to stimulate skin rejuvenation, improve texture, and reduce signs of aging.

Most clients experience minimal discomfort, often alleviated with a topical anesthetic before the procedure.

The number of sessions varies based on individual needs. Many clients see noticeable improvements after a series of treatments.

With regular sessions, you can expect improved skin texture, a reduction in fine lines, and a brighter, more youthful complexion.
