Post-op Physiotherapy

Book Now

Post-op Physiotherapy plays a critical role in your recovery journey after surgery. Our expert therapists design individualized rehabilitation programs aimed at restoring mobility, reducing pain, and rebuilding strength.

By incorporating gentle exercises, manual therapy, and functional training, our post-operative care helps prevent complications, minimizes recovery time, and improves overall surgical outcomes.

Key Benefits

With our tailored approach, you can experience:

  • Enhanced Mobility and Flexibility
  • Reduced Pain and Swelling
  • Faster Recovery and Healing
  • Strength and Endurance Restoration
  • Improved Functional Independence
  • Prevention of Post-Surgical Complications

Got Questions about Post-op Physiotherapy?

Our experienced therapists are here to answer your questions and provide guidance throughout your recovery.

The start time depends on your surgery and recovery plan. Typically, therapy begins once you are medically stable, often within a few days to a week post-surgery.

Our therapists conduct a comprehensive assessment to understand your specific needs and goals, allowing us to tailor a rehabilitation program that best supports your recovery.

Your therapy may include range-of-motion exercises, strengthening routines, balance training, and functional exercises designed to restore your daily activity levels.

Regular assessments and progress reviews with your therapist ensure that your rehabilitation program is effectively meeting your recovery goals.
