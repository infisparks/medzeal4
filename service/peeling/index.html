Chemical Peeling Treatments

At MedZeal, our medical facial treatments, performed under advanced chemical therapy, are designed to deeply exfoliate the skin, remove dead cells, and promote skin renewal. Our specialized procedures are tailored to address individual skincare concerns, ensuring safe and effective results.

We offer a range of medical facials under chemical therapy, including the MedZeal Facial, ExoLuxe Facial, Snail MediFacial, and Vampire Facial.
