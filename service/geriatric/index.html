Geriatric Physiotherapy

Book Now

Our Geriatric Physiotherapy program is specifically designed for older adults, focusing on improving mobility, balance, and strength. Through personalized rehabilitation plans, we aim to enhance quality of life and promote independent living.

By integrating gentle exercises, manual therapy, and balance training, our experienced therapists help reduce pain, prevent falls, and improve overall functional ability, ensuring a safer and more active lifestyle.

Key Benefits

With our tailored Geriatric Physiotherapy services, you can expect:

  • Improved Mobility and Flexibility
  • Enhanced Balance and Coordination
  • Pain Reduction and Management
  • Prevention of Falls
  • Increased Muscle Strength
  • Better Functional Independence
  • Customized Therapy Programs
  • Ongoing Progress Monitoring

Got Questions about Geriatric Physiotherapy?

Our expert therapists are here to answer your queries and guide you through our personalized rehabilitation programs.

Geriatric Physiotherapy is a specialized rehabilitation program designed to address the unique mobility and functional challenges faced by older adults.

Our therapy improves balance, strength, and flexibility, reducing pain and minimizing the risk of falls, thereby enhancing your independence.

We address issues such as arthritis, balance disorders, muscle weakness, and postural instability, along with rehabilitation after falls or surgery.

Regular assessments and follow-ups with your therapist ensure that your rehabilitation plan is adjusted to best meet your recovery goals.
